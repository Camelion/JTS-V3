Tablet of Vision:
You feel something enter your body and combine with energy that was already waiting inside. At that moment, another vision unfolds.
You feel the joy, pain, anger and love of the countless people who challenged this power.
Their emotions are like an overwhelming flood, and you scream.
But it is a scream within your mind without your mouth opening.
You are swept away by these other feelings, and your own awareness slowly disappears.
...
...
The awareness that didn't belong to you returns.
Your mouth moves without your knowing.
"Is this the end?"
