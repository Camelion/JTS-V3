Magister Winonin:
In order to bestow your weapon with a special ability, you must have a certain level of magic-filled souls. Collect the monster souls with the soul crystal that I gave you and bring it to the blacksmith. He will bestow a special ability on the weapon with the magic power of the souls.
I have already spoken with all the blacksmiths in each village, so all you have to do is bring them the collected souls.
Be warned, the monsters whose souls can be collected are located in Northern Oren, the Forest of Mirrors, Giant's Cave, the Devastated Castle, the Tower of Insolence upper level, Watcher's Tomb, the Garden of Eva, and Devil's Isle. If you can use a weapon that is bestowed with the collected souls, you will find you are much stronger!
