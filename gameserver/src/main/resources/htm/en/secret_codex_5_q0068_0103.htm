Tablet of Vision:
Light from the Tablet begins to engulf your body. Suddenly, it feels as if your blood is boiling. Another seal has been broken! But one more seal remains...
At that moment, knowledge from the Tablet enters your mind and a scene unfolds before you of an angel and what looks like another Tablet of Vision.
It seems to be located somewhere in the northwest part of the Forest of the Dead.
The light from the Tablet fades away.
