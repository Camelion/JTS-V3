Event Participation Method:
While hunting monsters in Lineage 2, you have a chance of obtaining items marked with letters or numbers. Collect a certain combination of them and bring them to me, and I will exchange them for prizes!
There are a total of 12 letters and numbers:5, Y, E, A, R, S, L, I, N, G, C, II
These letters and numbers can be combined to make the words LINEAGEII, 5YEARS and GRACIA. The prizes are different for each combination. Different prizes may be awarded even for the same combinations.
