There are a couple of things you should know. When you equip a Redemption Bow, you can use both Pardon and Forgiveness. I've put all my meager magical talents into these two abilities, and I'm confident that you'll find them very useful. You can use the Pardon once per minute; It is much more effective than Forgiveness, I've discovered.
Oh, and once in a rare while, some golden cursed pigs may shower you with special coins. Keep that in mind!
