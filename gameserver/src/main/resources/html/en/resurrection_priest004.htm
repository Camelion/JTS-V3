Priest of Resurrection:
If you must know, "Books of Resurrection" are 10 ancient books that reflects ones own courage. But, how does one acquire a "Book of Resurrection" you ask? Collect 100 "Proofs of Resurrection" to make 1 "Book of Resurrection". You are going to ask where to get "Proofs of Resurrection" aren't you? Through the spoils of monster hunting of course. It is not necessary to collect 1000 Proofs of Resurrection through hunting as opportunities to acquire Books of Resurrection may cross your path in your adventures. All you need to know is to collect 10 Books of Resurrection to complete the set.
