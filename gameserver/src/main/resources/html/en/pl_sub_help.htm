Any clan with whose level is 6 or above may establish a Royal Guard for 5000 Clan Rep points.
You can create up to 2 Royal Guards. Each can hold up to 20 clan members. Any clan whose level reaches 11 may increase the number of members in the Royal Guard for 7500 Clan Rep points.
Upon creation of a Royal Guard, 1 Royal Guard Captain must be appointed from the clan members. The class of the newly-appointed Royal Guard Captain will be increased.
