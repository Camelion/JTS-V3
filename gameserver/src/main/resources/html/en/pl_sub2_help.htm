Any clan with whose level is 7 or above may establish an Order of Knights for 10,000 Clan Rep points.
You can create up to 4 Orders of Knights. Each can accept up to 10 clan members. Any clan whose level reaches 9 may increase the number of members in the Knights for 5000 Clan Rep points. Up to level 10, an additional 2 Orders can be created.
Upon creation of an Order of Knights, 1 Captain must be appointed from among its members. The class of the newly-appointed Captain will be increased.
