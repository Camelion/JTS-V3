Tablet of Vision:
As you draw closer to the tablet, you can feel someone else's presence, one who made a deal with an evil force in trade for invincibility. Someone who offered his body to the devil for just one loss in order to learn the greatest skill.
People praise him for his swordsmanship, but he realized his limitations much too quickly, and he threw himself upon the easier path.
Countless thoughts pass through your mind. You remember the book you carry that talks about the poor swordsman. Did you read it? Can you really bring down the swordsman?
Defeat him!
