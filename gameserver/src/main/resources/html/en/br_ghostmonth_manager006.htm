The Soul Scent ability is as follows.
When using a monster's corpse, it comforts the monster's soul and let's you borrow the monster's power temporarily. For 2 minutes, it increases Max HP by 40%, Max MP by 40%, Max CP by 40%, physical P. Def. by 30%, M. Def. by 30%, moving speed by 20, and M. Atk. by 45%. It also decreases MP consumption during skill use by 10%.
