Mysterious Wizard:
This is the Forest of the Dead, a dwelling place for restless souls who died violent or sudden deaths. As a Dark Wizard, I found this place to be a fertile area for my studies. Injustice, resentment, death, hatred -- all the ingredients necessary for the Dark Arts are plentiful here.
If you wander around here, you may even encounter some of my puppets. Be warned! They are quite dangerous, and you would be well advised to avoid them.
